About Deoghar

Deoghar, also known as Baidyanath Dham, is a sacred city in the state of Jharkhand, India. It is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and is a significant pilgrimage site for Hindus. The city is known for its temples, serene environment, and rich cultural heritage.

Tourist Attractions

Baidyanath Temple

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas, this temple is the most famous attraction in Deoghar.

Naulakha Mandir

A beautiful temple dedicated to Radha-Krishna, resembling the Belur Math in West Bengal.

Tapovan

A serene place with hot springs and beautiful scenery, perfect for meditation and relaxation.

Transport Facilities

By Air

The nearest airport is Deoghar Airport (DGH), which is well-connected to major cities like Kolkata, Patna, and Ranchi.

By Train

Deoghar has its own railway station, Baidyanath Dham Junction (BDME), which is connected to major cities like Kolkata, Delhi, and Mumbai.

By Road

Deoghar is well-connected by road. Regular bus services are available from nearby cities like Ranchi, Patna, and Kolkata.
